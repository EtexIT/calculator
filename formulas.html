Financial Calculations Explained

This page provides detailed explanations of all formulas used in the business case calculations. Understanding these formulas helps in making informed decisions about project investments.

Project Costs

Team Costs

Per role calculation:

Team Cost = Daily Rate × Number of Days × Number of Persons × (1 + Contingency%)

Total team cost is the sum across all roles and phases (Validation, Preparation, Execution).

Technology Costs

One-time costs:

One-time Tech Cost = Sum of all one-time technology investments

Recurring costs:

Recurring Tech Cost = Monthly Cost × Number of Months

Risk Adjustment

Based on Project Assessment Framework score:

Risk Adjusted Cost = Base Cost × (1 + Risk Adjustment%)
  • Score 8-13: 10% adjustment
  • Score 14-19: 15% adjustment
  • Score 20-24: 20% adjustment
  • Score 25+: 25% adjustment

Benefits & Savings

One-off Savings

Applied in Year 1 after project completion:

Total One-off Savings = Sum of all one-time savings entries

Recurring Savings

Calculated per year:

Yearly Recurring Savings = Sum of all recurring savings for that year

Financial Metrics

Return on Investment (ROI)

Calculated over the 5-year period:

ROI = ((Total Benefits - Total Costs) / Total Costs) × 100%

Where Total Benefits = One-off Savings + Sum of Recurring Savings

Payback Period

Calculated by tracking accumulated net benefits:

Accumulated Net Benefit = Sum of (Benefits - Costs) per year

Payback period is reached when Accumulated Net Benefit becomes positive. Fractional years are calculated by linear interpolation.

Net Cash Flow

Calculated per year:

Net Cash Flow = (One-off Savings + Recurring Savings) - (Project Costs + Recurring Costs)

Accumulated Benefit

Running total of net cash flows:

Accumulated Benefit = Sum of Net Cash Flows up to current year

Cost Distribution

Timeline Distribution

Project costs are front-loaded:

  • All one-time costs (team, technology, external) are applied in Year 1
  • Risk adjustment is applied to Year 1 costs
  • Only recurring technology costs are distributed across years
  • Benefits start accruing after project completion

Recurring technology costs distribution:

Yearly Recurring Cost = Total Recurring Cost / 5
